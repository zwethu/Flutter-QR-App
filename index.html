Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to QR Helper. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our application ("QR Helper").

1. Information We Collect

QR Helper does not collect or store any personal information from its users. The app is designed to function without the need for any user data collection.

2. Permissions

QR Helper requires the following permissions:

Camera Permission: This permission is necessary to scan QR codes using your device's camera. We do not collect, store, or share any images or data captured by the camera.

3. How We Use Information

As QR Helper does not collect any personal information, we do not use any information for analytics, marketing, or other purposes. The app solely processes data locally on your device to generate and scan QR codes.

4. Data Handling

QR Code Generation: The app allows you to generate QR codes from various string inputs, such as URLs, links, and text. This data is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to any server or third party.

QR Code Scanning: The app uses your device's camera to scan QR codes and display the resulting string. This data is processed locally and is not shared with any server or third party.

Copy to Clipboard: Users have the option to copy the scanned string to their device's clipboard. This action is performed locally on your device.

5. Third-Party Services

QR Helper does not use any third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, please be aware that no method of transmitting data over the internet or storing data electronically can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
